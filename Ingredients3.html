The Ingredients for Semisweet Chocolate Mousse are as follows:
  • 1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips (₹100)
  • 1 tablespoon water (₹2)
  • 1 large egg yolk, lightly beaten (₹4)
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract (₹20)
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream (₹50)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (₹1)
  • Optional: Whipped cream and raspberries ₹50 (20g of whipped cream₹15+ 5 raspberries ₹35=₹50)
