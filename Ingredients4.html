The Ingredients for Cream of Mushroom are as follows:
  • 2 tablespoons butter (₹5)
  • 1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms (₹20)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion (₹5)
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour (₹100)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (₹1)
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper (₹5)
  • 2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) chicken broth(₹50)
  • 1 cup half-and-half cream (₹25)
