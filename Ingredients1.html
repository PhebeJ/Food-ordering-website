Ingredients for Caramelised onion tarte Tatin are as follows:

  • 20g butter (₹10)
  • 1 tsp olive oil (₹25)
  • 2 red onions, thickly sliced lengthways (₹ 11)
  • 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced (₹3)
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar (₹10)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp red wine vinegar (₹ 25)
  • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry, just thawed (₹20)
  • 50g goat’s cheese, crumbled (₹190)
